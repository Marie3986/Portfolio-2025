About Me

Hi, I'm a UX designer who's all about turning challenges into simple, human-centered solutions. I believe the best designs come from curiosity, asking questions, listening closely, and always keeping people at the heart of the process. For me, design is less about shiny visuals and more about creating experiences that actually make life easier (though I do love a clean, elegant interface).

Outside of projects and prototypes, you'll probably find me recharging at the gym or diving into a good book. Both help me stay balanced, inspired, and ready to bring fresh ideas into my design work. In short: I'm passionate about crafting digital experiences people love to use, and I'm always looking for new ways to grow and explore as a designer.
